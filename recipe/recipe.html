Lemonade Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6-8 cups cold water to taste
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh lemon juice
  • 2-3 lemons sliced thin
  • fresh mint
  • ice

Insturctions

  1. Combine 2 cups water and sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook until the sugar dissolves, stirring several times. Cool to room temperature.
  2. Combine with the fresh lemon juice and remaining water. Add lemon slices or fresh mint and plenty of ice.
